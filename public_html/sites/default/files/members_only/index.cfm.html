Member Login

Please enter your username and password.

Username: Your membership ID number. (Disregard any zeros that may appear at the beginning of your membership ID number.)

Password: Your last name is your password. Be sure to capitalize the first letter of your last name as the field is case sensitive. If you have a space in your last name such as Mac Donald, eliminate the space and type MacDonald

Example: If your SAF membership number is 000315 and your last name is Robertson, you would type: 315 for the "Username" Robertson for the "Password"
